About The Project

Designing a web based portal to monitor real time crop Health by using deep learning. Farmer needs to upload the images of their crop to diagnose the disease and monitor crop health. The model will use image recognition and deep learning in background to identify the crop disease , weeds and other crop related problems. And will suggest proper Pesticides and weedicides. This will make the farmers independent and help them to select proper Nutrition and guidance to grow crops properly.
